Air-Head Balloon Ride

Step on up to take a ride on our Air Head Balloon! On this ride we will fly over the river, where you can watch fellow candylandians swim with the swedish fish. When it's time for the ride to be over, you will have two options you can either parachute out and land on Jelly Belly Island, or take a more calm descent and land in Butterfinger Village.
